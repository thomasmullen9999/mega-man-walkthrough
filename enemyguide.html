Enemy Guide - Mega Man 1 Guide

  • Introduction
  • Walkthrough
  • Enemy Guide

This page details the regular enemies that you'll find dotted in the different levels throughout the game. There are 19 in total mentioned here. If you're looking for information on how to defeat bosses, check their respective level in the main walkthrough and scroll down to the bottom of the page for boss guides; this page is specifically for the more common, lower-level enemies. The enemies are arranged in alphabetical order.

Here is an example of what the attributes in each green box mean. Consider it a template for the actual enemy files below.

Enemy's Name

  • Also known as: These are some of the other names given to the enemy in official English translations of the game (eg. in remakes/re-releases).
  • Appears in: These are the levels in the game that the enemy appears in.
  • HP: How many shots the enemy will take to kill when using the default weapon, the Mega Buster.
  • Attack: How much damage the enemy can deal to Mega Man; the number of units his health bar will decrease by.
  • Weakness: The weapon most effective at defeating this enemy.
  • Difficulty: Self-explanatory; how hard/annoying the enemy is to defeat/get past. Ranked out of five stars.
  • Description: A brief paragraph with information on how to deal with the enemy.

Thanks to the Mega Man Legacy Collection for detailing the HP, Attack and Weakness stats for me so I didn't have to spend a lot of time finding them myself by playing the game. Without further ado...

Adhering Suzy

  • Also known as: Octopus Battery, Octo Battery
  • Appears in: Cut Man's stage, Ice Man's stage, Bomb Man's stage, Wily's Fortress 2 & 3
  • HP: 05
  • Attack: 04
  • Weakness: Thunder Beam
  • Difficulty: ★ ★ ☆ ☆ ☆
  • Description: These things always travel in a straight line, either vertically or horizontally. Watch their movements from nearby before going in to attack, so you know where you'll be safe to shoot them from.

Big Eye

  • Also known as: N/A
  • Appears in: Guts Man's stage, Cut Man's stage, Elec Man's stage, Ice Man's stage, Wily's Fortress 1
  • HP: 20
  • Attack: 10
  • Weakness: Super Arm
  • Difficulty: ★ ★ ★ ★ ★
  • Description: You'll often see these at the end of a level before the boss (or see three together at the start of the first Wily stage). They take a lot of shots to kill regardless of which weapon you use and you won't have much time as they jump towards you quickly. Instead of defeating it, use the Ice Slasher to freeze it in midair and slip on under. This is probably the most annoying and tricky regular enemy.

Blaster

  • Also known as: Beak
  • Appears in: Cut Man's stage, Bomb Man's stage, Wily's Fortress 2
  • HP: 01
  • Attack: 01
  • Weakness: Mega Buster
  • Difficulty: ★ ☆ ☆ ☆ ☆
  • Description: Blasters are small cannon-guns stuck to walls that shoot laser spheres in 4 directions. They shoot in the same pattern every time so you should watch them from afar if possible to see where their shots are going to land, then stand in a safe place. Shoot them where their cannon opens up and they become vulnerable.

Bombombomb

  • Also known as: Bombomb
  • Appears in: Bomb Man's stage, Wily's Fortress 2
  • HP: 00
  • Attack: 01
  • Weakness: N/A
  • Difficulty: ★ ★ ★ ☆ ☆
  • Description: These are only seen for short periods in the levels they appear in. They are invincible so don't bother shooting them and just try to avoid the bits of shrapnel they explode into. For more detail on dealing with these guys, check the walkthrough section for Bomb Man's stage.

Bunby Heli

  • Also known as: Blader
  • Appears in: Guts Man's stage, Cut Man's stage, Wily's Fortress 2
  • HP: 01
  • Attack: 03
  • Weakness: Mega Buster
  • Difficulty: ★ ★ ☆ ☆ ☆
  • Description: These guys are very annoying and swoop down rapidly when they're above you. Either run past them and possibly take a little damage or jump and shoot them at the precise moment they start to come down. The Thunder Beam is effective as it fires up above you as well as straight ahead.

Changkey

  • Also known as: Tackle Fire
  • Appears in: Fire Man's stage
  • HP: 01
  • Attack: 02
  • Weakness: Mega Buster
  • Difficulty: ★ ★ ☆ ☆ ☆
  • Description: These hotheads emerge suddenly from pits in sets of three. Be careful they don't burn you as you jump over the pits, and then keep an eye to the sky as they'll then descend from above and float down in different paths.

Crazy Razy

  • Also known as: N/A
  • Appears in: Ice Man's stage
  • HP: 03
  • Attack: 03
  • Weakness: Thunder Beam
  • Difficulty: ★ ★ ★ ☆ ☆
  • Description: These only appear near the beginning of Ice Man's stage. To defeat them, simply shoot their head once. If you shoot their legs, their head will detach and start attacking you like an large Bunby Heli; this can be really annoying.

Foot Holder

  • Also known as: N/A
  • Appears in: Ice Man's stage, Wily's Fortress 1
  • HP: 00
  • Attack: 03
  • Weakness: N/A
  • Difficulty: ★ ★ ★ ★ ☆
  • Description: Foot Holders are more like stage hazards than enemies, as you can't defeat them but they can hurt you by shooting out small beams from their sides. You need to use them as platforms to cross a bottomless pit or a gorge of spikes. Their erratic and unpredictable movements make them annoying to jump between, so it may be better to use the Magnet Beam (if you have it) to cross the pits in the two areas where Foot Holders are situated.

Gabyoall

  • Also known as: Gabyool, Spine
  • Appears in: Elec Man's stage, Ice Man's stage, Fire Man's stage, Bomb Man's stage
  • HP: 01
  • Attack: 03
  • Weakness: Thunder Beam
  • Difficulty: ★ ★ ★ ☆ ☆
  • Description: These things look like spike traps from Zelda, and act in a very similar way. They move back and forth along the floor, and will speed up when you are standing near them. They'll often knock you off ledges and are invincible to the Mega Buster, so use the Thunder Beam or Rolling Cutter to destroy and make things much easier.

Kamadoma

  • Also known as: Flea
  • Appears in: Cut Man's stage, Elec Man's stage, Bomb Man's stage, Wily's Fortress 1
  • HP: 01
  • Attack: 02
  • Weakness: Mega Buster
  • Difficulty: ★ ☆ ☆ ☆ ☆
  • Description: Kamadomas jump around very eagerly like robotic crickets. They're very simple enemies and, although they can sometimes be pests that are hard to hit, they die from one shot from the Mega Buster.

Killer Bomb

  • Also known as: Killer Bullet
  • Appears in: Fire Man's stage, Bomb Man's stage, Wily's Fortress 1, Wily's Fortress 3
  • HP: 01
  • Attack: 03
  • Weakness: Mega Buster
  • Difficulty: ★ ★ ☆ ☆ ☆
  • Description: The long lost brother of Bullet Bill from Mario games? They'll fly towards you in a wave-shaped pattern. Try to shoot them from afar as they die in only one shot but explode upon being hit, potentially damaging you.

Mambu

  • Also known as: Flying Shell
  • Appears in: Cut Man's stage, Bomb Man's stage
  • HP: 01
  • Attack: 01
  • Weakness: Mega Buster
  • Difficulty: ★ ★ ★ ☆ ☆
  • Description: These enemies release 8 beam shots in a circle around them that can be tricky to dodge depending on where they are in the level. They do die in only one shot but it may be safer to just run past them, especially if they're above a spike pit.

Metall

  • Also known as: Met
  • Appears in: Gut Man's stage
  • HP: 01
  • Attack: 01
  • Weakness: Mega Buster
  • Difficulty: ★ ☆ ☆ ☆ ☆
  • Description: Although they appear in only one stage, I would say these are the Goombas of this game, and the Mega Man series in general. One shot will kill them, and their three shots are easy to dodge. Just fire when it lifts up its helmet.

Pepe

  • Also known as: Peng
  • Appears in: Ice Man's stage, Wily's Fortress 3
  • HP: 01
  • Attack: 04
  • Weakness: Mega Buster
  • Difficulty: ★ ★ ☆ ☆ ☆
  • Description: They're very simple to kill but their wavy movements make them annoying to hit. Definitely not hard enemies by any stretch, but they're a pain if they knock you into a spike pit. Use the Ice Slasher to freeze them in midair?

Pickelman

  • Also known as: Picket Man
  • Appears in: Guts Man's stage
  • HP: 10
  • Attack: 03
  • Weakness: Fire Storm
  • Difficulty: ★ ★ ★ ☆ ☆
  • Description: These guys take quite a few hits. What you want to do is wait until they lift their shield, then fire at them rapidly and move in slowly to avoid their raining pickaxes that they throw at you. Hopefully you can kill them before you run into them with this method. However, use Fire Storm or Hyper Bomb if you have them because they kill Pickelmen in one hit.

Screw Driver

  • Also known as: Screw Bomber
  • Appears in: Cut Man's stage, Fire Man's stage, Wily's Fortress 2, Wily's Fortress 3, Wily's Fortress 4
  • HP: 03
  • Attack: 02
  • Weakness: Thunder Beam
  • Difficulty: ★ ★ ☆ ☆ ☆
  • Description: One of the most common enemies in the game. They can be found on both the floor and ceiling, and when they emerge they will shoot 5 beams that spread and travel in different directions. They're annoying when there are a lot of them (particularly in the last level) but watch for where their shots land and you'll be fine.

Sniper Joe

  • Also known as: N/A
  • Appears in: Bomb Man's stage
  • HP: 10
  • Attack: 04
  • Weakness: Hyper Bomb
  • Difficulty: ★ ★ ★ ★ ☆
  • Description: The first robot in the 'Joe' series that come in lots of different variations throughout the series. These guys are similar to the Pickelmen, except they shoot you with a gun arm just like Mega Man's. They'll jump around a lot too. Shoot at them when they lift their shields and have patience. Fun fact: Sniper Joe was created by Dr Light and is based on the design of Proto Man, a popular recurring character who makes his debut in Mega Man 3.

Super Cutter

  • Also known as: N/A
  • Appears in: Cut Man's stage
  • HP: 05
  • Attack: 04
  • Weakness: Hyper Bomb
  • Difficulty: ★ ☆ ☆ ☆ ☆
  • Description: These things look like white versions of Pac-Man but they're actually sentient versions of Cut Man's weapon. They only appear in two parts of Cut Man's stage and all they do is jump out of silver cages at you. They're indestructible to most weapons but all you need to do is move right past their cages without stopping; they pose no real threat.

Watcher

  • Also known as: N/A
  • Appears in: Elec Man's stage, Wily's Fortress 4
  • HP: 01
  • Attack: 04
  • Weakness: Mega Buster
  • Difficulty: ★ ★ ★ ☆ ☆
  • Description: Watchers will move onto the screen in groups from above or below and shoot out electric beams from their eyes when you're in front of them. These beams can be a major hindrance if they knock you off a ladder and cause you to fall to the screen below. Shoot left/right at them as soon as they're parallel to you (one hit kills them).
